## 👋 About me

I'm a full-stack **AI builder** and technical co-founder. For 7+ years I've taken products from a one-sentence idea to a live, revenue-generating app — designing the UX, building the frontend and backend, and wiring in the AI that makes them worth using. Mostly solo. I build with AI in the loop every day, and I'd rather ship a working prototype this week than spec it for a quarter.

> 🔒 Most of my production work lives in private/client repos. The links below are **live products you can actually use** — and the pinned repos are open-source demos of how I build.
